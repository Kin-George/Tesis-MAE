Literature Review

Literature Review

  • They find, after the outbreak of terrorism in the late 1960's, per capita GDP in the Basque Country declined about 10 percentage points relative to a synthetic control region without terrorism. (Abadie & Gardeazabal, 2003)
  • They construct a synthetic control for Turkey and by comparing the behavior of the GDP per capita of synthetic Turkey without terrorism with the real Turkey they find that in the period 1988-2008 the GDP per capita of synthetic Turkey would have been on average $1585 higher per year, translating into an average increase of 13.8% in GDP per capita. (Bilgel & Karahasan, 2016)
  • They construct a synthetic control to isolate the effect of reunification in West Germany and find that reunification resulted in a reduction of $1600 per year on average during 1990 - 2003. (Abadie, Diamond & Hainmueller, 2015)
  • He constructs a synthetic control to isolate the effect of Hugo Chavez's presidency on the behavior of key indicators of the Venezuelan economy, and finds that had Chavez not been elected, the Venezuelan economy would still have declined but not as deeply. (Maynard, 2015)
